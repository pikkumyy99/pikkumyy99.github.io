Calcifer 2023

Tool: Blender

Created Calcifer from Howl's Moving Castle by Ghibli. Fairly simple, but during the creation process I got to test and familiarise myself with many tools Blender has to offer, including combining vertex groups and particle systems, smoke domain and displace.

Easter Dreamies 2023

Tool: Blender

Small Easter scene to celebrate Easter and spring. I wanted to create something colorful and test water animation as well. Isometric creations from others inspired for this camera angle, even though it is not completely accurate.

Peek-A-Boo 2023

Tool: Blender

Small nature scene inspired by Ghibli. I tried to create similar shaders seen in Ghibli movies. The first dive into different style compared to previous ones.

Silent Night 2022
